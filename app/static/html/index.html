This is a minimal viable product for the Quiz Widget concept. Please report bugs.

No design or product review has been done yet - so I would love to hear from you how this product could be better.

If you have design ideas, preferred features, or general product ideas for how to make this more usable and better suit you, please email me at alexandra.berke@huffingtonpost.com

Requested Enhancements:

  • Broader sharing options.

  • Ability to edit quizzes

  • Ability to create questions with more than 2 answers.

  • More exciting outcome transition.

  • Ability to go back and edit quizzes.

  • "Retake" button

  • "Previous" button to go back to previous question

  • Ability to weight outcomes
